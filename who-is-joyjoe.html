Who is Joy Joe?

Passionate Balloon Artist in Brisbane

I learned the balloon twisting skill when I was at University. I actually joined a Magic Group as I wanted to learn some magic tricks. At that time, I volunteered helping children's assignments. Children love me as I always show them new tricks.

One day, my magic teacher taught me a new trick, which uses the balloon puppet dog to find an audience chosen card! I then fell in love with this new talent.

Ten years ago, I thought that maybe making balloon animals for parties is a good idea. I decided to turn my passion into a business. Now, I have been to many birthday parties and corporate events. Looking forward to seeing you!

How I Improve My Skills

I actually joined Balloon Artist College to upgrade my magic power. Yes! There is a Balloon Artist College. It teaches advanced balloon designs and the principles of creating your own designs. I also attend local artist meetups to make friends with other artists and share information about balloon making trends.

What I Love About Twisting Balloons at Birthday Parties

Hey! When I first started, I was surprised by watching kids hitting a horse toy. Yeah, I now know it's called Piñatas. In my country, Taiwan, we don't have these kinds of activities. I feel blessed as I am able to see how different cultures celebrate their birthday parties. I have tried different foods and desserts. Plus, I also enjoy watching the little happy faces when they get my balloons.
